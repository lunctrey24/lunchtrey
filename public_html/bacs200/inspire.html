Odell Beckham Jr.

Odell Beckham Jr. is one of the most criticized athletes in all of sports today, but I find him very inspiring. As an athlete, I know the pressure he feels, but I know he has a tremendous more amount than I do, but I can relate.

OBJ shows me how to enjoy the game everytime you're out on the field, no matter what the noise around him is saying about him. Afterall, football still is just a game and it should always be fun.
